Hi, I'm Quinn

Hydrologist and Data Scientist

I am a water scientist specializing in the collection, simulation, management, and analysis of hydrologic information based out of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Since 2014, I've worked and volunteered at places like Tetra Tech, GeoSystems Analysis, the US Geological Survey, the University of Arizona and Caminos del Agua. Some highlights from my work experiences are

  • helping to develop modeling solutions for the hydrology of the Colorado River
  • leading multi-year field studies of contaminated hydrologic sites in Massachusetts, USA
  • creating and managing data pipelines for high-dimensional hydrologic datasets
  • designing web content and educational curriculum to make hydrologic information available to the public
